Biography

I am currently a doctoral student at Deep Imaging Group, College of Computer Science, Sichuan University, China, under the supervision of Prof. Yi Zhang. Before that, I received my M.S. degree from College of Computer Science, Sichuan University, China, in 2020, under the supervision of Prof. Yi Zhang, and B.E. degree from College of Software Engineering, Sichuan University, China, in 2017.

Research Interests

I work in the field of image processing, specializing in accelerate MRI Reconstruction. Recently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Inverse Problems
  • Medical Imaging
  • Image Processing
